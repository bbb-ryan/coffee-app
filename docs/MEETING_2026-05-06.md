## Agenda

### 1. Why we're here (5 min)

What changes today: we're done with the exploration phase. Phases 1–4 taught us what the app could be. Phase 5 is one focused thing — making the experience personal — and we're going to work in a more structured way to actually ship it.

Quick check-in: does that framing land? Any objections before we go further?

### 2. Hassan walks through CONCEPT-V2 (15 min)

Hassan presents the moments framework (decode / recommend / depth) and personas (Noor / Mika / Dev) from his branch. Group decides:

- **Adopt as Phase 5 backbone?** Yes / merge with personalization frame / table.
- **What from `hassan-design/` makes it into `prototypes/` for active use?**
- **Which of Hassan's flows do we prototype first?** (Decode-a-bag, recommendation loop, depth moment, café mode — pick 1–2.)

Decision captured in `docs/PHASE_5_VISION.md` v2 after the meeting.

### 3. Personalization, concretely (15 min)

Working from the vision doc's "Personalization, concretely" section:

- **Profile inputs.** Are we comfortable using metadata (location, device, time) as personalization signals from day one? What's our consent posture?
- **First three personalized surfaces.** The doc proposes: homepage hero, bean detail "why this for you," diary depth prompts. Agree, swap, or add.
- **Account model for M1.** localStorage-only, or invest in Supabase + auth now? (Tradeoff is in the vision doc.)

Decision: pick **three surfaces** and **one account model** for M1.

### 4. What we keep, change, or sunset (10 min)

Walk the table in `docs/PHASE_5_VISION.md` ("What we keep, change, or sunset"). For each row, vote:

- **Keep** as-is, no work
- **Evolve** in Phase 5
- **Audit** — decide later
- **Sunset** — hide or remove

Specifically debate: social feed, magazine, brew guide, Marco the Barista. These are the ambiguous ones.

### 5. New ways of working (10 min)

Walk through `docs/WORKFLOW.md` together. Specifically:

- **Branching.** Short-lived branches, named `your-name/feature`. No more long-running personal kingdoms.
- **PRs.** Required for every change, even tiny. Reviewer pulls and runs UI changes.
- **File structure.** New `prototypes/` folder for design exploration; new `archive/` for old work. Nothing loose at the root.
- **Documentation.** Decisions go in `docs/` or they didn't happen.

Open the floor: what won't work? What needs adjusting?

### 6. M1 owners (5 min)

M1 (Profile foundation, ~2 weeks) tasks from the vision doc:

| Task | Suggested owner |
|---|---|
| Define `TasteProfile` data model + types | Engineer-leaning person |
| `TasteProfileProvider` (mirrors `DiaryProvider`) | Same person, or pair |
| Metadata capture decisions + consent posture write-up | Researcher / PM-leaning person |
| `/profile/debug` inspector page | Designer + engineer pair |
| Cleanup PR: archive Phase 1–4 prototypes per `WORKFLOW.md` | Volunteer (probably Emily, this is partly done as of the kickoff) |

Names assigned live. Each owner opens a draft PR within 48 hours.
