Why this bean You haven't tried this one, but you should. Heart sources from small Kenyan lots and roasts light to preserve origin character. The acidity here is more savory than the Ethiopian — blackcurrant and grapefruit instead of blueberry. Same freshness standards, new territory.

The Stretch

Antigua Pastores

Guatemala · Stumptown · Dark Roast

03
Why this bean This is outside your usual zone — but hear us out. Stumptown's Antigua is roasted dark and ships fast from their Portland roastery. Freshness Chasers who've tried it rated it 4.3/5. The body is rich without being ashy, and the chocolate-almond profile is a different kind of beautiful through a Chemex.
Acidity
Soft, rounded
Body
Rich and velvety
Sweetness
Dark chocolate, molasses
chocolate almond brown sugar cedar

Chemex tip: Drop your water temp to 198°F — dark roasts extract faster and you don't want bitterness. Slightly coarser grind. The Chemex paper filter will keep it clean even with the heavier body.
