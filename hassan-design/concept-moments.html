Coffee Decoder · Concept V2

The user lives in three moments.

Not three audiences. One person, moving through discovery, purchase, and depth — in different proportions, as they grow. The app recognizes the moment and serves accordingly.

The three personas

N

Noor · Curious Home Brewer

V60 on the counter, one bag a month. The main character. Lives in all three moments.

M

Mika · Thoughtful Outsider

Doesn't drink coffee. Buying a bag for her partner. Almost entirely in the purchase moment.

D

Dev · Roaster's Apprentice

Learning to cup. Lives in the depth moment constantly. Power user, not the primary target.

How to read this map

The app doesn't switch modes between people. It switches emphasis between moments. Noor's home screen in January (discovery) looks different from her home screen in September (depth) — but every flow has to work for her across all three. Mika's single purchase flow has to work for Noor too. Dev's depth layer has to stay out of the way when Noor isn't ready for it.
